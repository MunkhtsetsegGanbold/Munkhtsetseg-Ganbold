# Munkhtsetseg
This page is where my life’s passions come together.
I am a mother who loves learning and believes knowledge grows stronger when shared. I enjoy studying alongside my daughter and discovering practical, thoughtful ways to teach children while continuing to learn ourselves. Here, I share what I read, how I learn, and how families can grow together through education.
I also write about my professional journey, including my path toward PMP certification, project management insights, and lessons learned along the way.
Climate action is a core part of my identity. As a member of Climate Cardinals, a global youth-led NGO, I focus on raising awareness and empowering young people—especially in Mongolia—where climate challenges are deeply felt. My goal is to help the youth generation learn, take initiative, and become leaders who shape a sustainable future.
This is a space for learning, motherhood, career growth, and climate responsibility — all parts of one meaningful life.
